Introduction to Crystals

Why Are Crystals Used?

Going back to ancient times, in places like Greece, Eqypt and China, crystals have been used with the belief they held healing properties for the mind, body, and soul. Whilst there currently is no scientific evidence confirming the healing properties of crystals, those who do believe understand crystals to carry energies that can be transferred to us and help to rid negative energies.

There are said to be many properties held by crystals including protection, abundance, love and wisdom. Some crystals are also said to have energy amplifying properties, such as clear quartz, and are used with the intention of amplifying positive energies surrounding a person or an environment. How Are Crystals Used?

There are many different rituals practiced with the use of crystals including meditation, baths, alters, chakra cleanses and more. Different crystals will cary their own energy types and so the rituals completed will be deeply personal for everyone, providing a space for mindfullness and reflection. In essence, crystals are used as a form of self-care to de-stress and find clarity.

Some of the more common crystals used include, Amathyst, Citrine, Turquoise, Quartz, Jasper, Tigers Eye and many more. When obtaining your first crystals it is important to clease them as soon as possible in order to remove any energies still attached to the crystals from the previous owners. Depending on the type of crystal there are different methods that can be used to clease them, some crystals can be submerged in water and placed in the moonlight but not all. Those not able to be submerged in water can be cleansed through a process called Smudging, where sage is burnt and the crystals placed within the smoke.
